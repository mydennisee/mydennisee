## How I Work

1. **Frame the problem before touching the data.** Business context first — what decision does this model or system need to support?
2. **Architecture before code.** Design the end-to-end flow, identify failure points, align stakeholders — then build.
3. **Implement, measure, explain.** Every model output is explainable. SHAP, confidence scoring, audit trails — not optional.
4. **Iterate with a loop.** CRISP-DM, MLOps, AI tuning — all structured as feedback cycles, not one-shot deliveries.
5. **Own the outcome.** From whiteboard to boardroom. Design, build, present, sign off.
